# FL-03 — What Are You Proving?

## 📖 Overview

This assignment focuses on defining a clear proof statement by identifying:

- One specific professional claim
- One specific person I help
- One clear action I want visitors to take

Instead of asking AI to write the answer immediately, I used Claude AI to interview me with follow-up questions until my positioning became specific, truthful, and evidence-based.

## 📚 What I Learned

This assignment taught me that a strong portfolio is built around clarity rather than broad claims.

Instead of saying I build automation for "businesses," I learned to define:

- One specific claim
- One specific audience
- One clear call-to-action

This exercise helped me position my work around solving a real communication problem for a specific business owner while keeping every claim truthful and evidence-based.
